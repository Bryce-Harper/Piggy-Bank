# 🐷 Getting Piggy Bank on Your Phone
## A step-by-step guide (free, no credit card needed)

---

## What you'll need
- A computer (to set up once)
- A GitHub account (free) → github.com
- A Vercel account (free) → vercel.com

---

## Step 1 — Put the code on GitHub

1. Go to **github.com** and sign up / log in
2. Click the **+** button → "New repository"
3. Name it `piggy-bank`, make it **Public**, click "Create repository"
4. You'll see a page with a URL like `https://github.com/YOURNAME/piggy-bank`

Now upload the files:
1. Click **"uploading an existing file"** on the repo page
2. Drag ALL the project files/folders in (or use the file picker)
   - Make sure to upload the whole folder structure as-is
3. Click **"Commit changes"**

---

## Step 2 — Deploy to Vercel

1. Go to **vercel.com** and sign up with your GitHub account
2. Click **"Add New Project"**
3. Find your `piggy-bank` repo and click **"Import"**
4. Vercel auto-detects Vite — just click **"Deploy"**
5. Wait ~1 minute ✅

Vercel gives you a free URL like:
`https://piggy-bank-abc123.vercel.app`

That's your live app! Open it on your phone right now.

---

## Step 3 — Add to your home screen

### iPhone (Safari):
1. Open your Vercel URL in **Safari** (must be Safari)
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down → tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** → done! 🎉

### Android (Chrome):
1. Open your Vercel URL in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (three dots, top right)
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** → done! 🎉

---

## Your data
- Everything saves automatically to your phone's local storage
- Data stays on YOUR phone — nothing goes to a server
- If you clear your browser data, entries will be lost (so don't do that!)

---

## Making updates later
If you want to change something:
1. Edit the files in your GitHub repo (click a file → pencil icon)
2. Commit the change
3. Vercel automatically rebuilds and deploys in ~1 minute!
